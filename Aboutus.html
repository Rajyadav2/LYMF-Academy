About LYMF Academy

Laxmi Yoga Meditation and Fitness Academy is a multifaceted wellness center runned by Dr. Sunil Yadav. It is situated at Laxmi Kuteer Near B D Academy, Manorathpuri Colony, Susuwahi, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh 221011, India. This academy offers a variety of services including yoga, meditation, fitness training, Yoga Therapy, Ashtanga yoga, Power of Meditation, Power Yoga, Hath Yoga, Sehej Yog classes..
